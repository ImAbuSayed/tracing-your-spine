[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
The shadows stretching out across the wooden floor
Your silhouette is standing by the open door
A single spark is dancing in the fading light
I want to lose myself inside your eyes tonight

[Verse 1]
The embers burning low inside the open fire
Every breath is pulling us a little higher
A gentle breeze is blowing through the window pane
I want to wash away inside this midnight rain

[Pre-Chorus]
Lean a little closer in the quiet dark
Listen to the rhythm of my racing heart
Let the heavy world outside just fade away
There is nothing left that we could ever say

[Chorus]
I am tracing your spine in the candle glow
I am taking my time moving sweet and slow
Memorizing every line upon your skin
Letting all this beautiful fire begin

[Post-Chorus]
Moving sweet and slow, let the shadows fall
Tracing your spine, I want to feel it all

[Verse 2]
The radio is playing something soft and low
A melody we listened to so long ago
Your fingers find the collar of my faded shirt
This kind of heaven takes away the lonely hurt

[Bridge]
The shadows stretching out across the wooden floor
I want to hold you closer than I did before
Every single secret you have tried to hide
I am keeping safely right here by your side
There is a fever waking up inside my soul
And Saeed is the one who loses control
